Mt Rainier is an active volcano.

It has a history of catastrophic lahars -- Mega Mudflows from the Mountain.

You, your family, and your wider community may be at risk if you live, work, or go to school in the Lahar Zone.
Prepare now to protect yourself and your loved ones.

Use this website to:

Be Lahar Ready.

Follow these simple steps:

Be Aware.

Know the Hazard. Go to the Lahars page to learn about lahars and where in the Puget Sound Lowlands they might impact.

Be Prepared.

Know how to be ready. Go to the Prepare page to learn about the best steps to take to make sure you are ready to respond in the event of a lahar.

Be Safe - Beat it!

Know how to be safe. Go to the Evacuate page to learn about how to respond to a lahar, including which evacuation routes to take.
If you hear a siren or receive an alert, get out -- fast!
